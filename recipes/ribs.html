Barbecue Ribs

Description

This oven-baked baby back ribs is incredibly easy to make but delicious and full of flavor. It is seasoned with a dry rub, glazed with barbeque sauce and baked until fall-off-the-bone tender. Try it out with any combination of your favorite dry rub and barbecue sauce!

Ingredients

  • 1 rack of baby back ribs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 cups barbecue sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees F
  2. Mix salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and cumin in a bowl
  3. Prick the back of the rack of ribs with a fork or knife a few times
  4. Rub spice mix on all sides of the rack of ribs
  5. Place rack, meat side down, on aluminum foil and fold the foil over the ribs to create a tight seal
  6. Place on pan and bake for 2 hours
  7. Remove from oven and let the ribs rest for 15 minutes
  8. Meanwhile, increase oven temperature to 350 degrees F
  9. Open foil, drain any juices, and brush barbecue sauce on all sides of the ribs
  10. Turn rack so it is meat side up. Leave the foil open and bake for 10 minutes
  11. Remove from oven, brush another layer of barbecue sauce on only the meat, and bake for another 10 minutes
  12. Repeat previous step (brushing with barbecue sauce and baking 10 minutes) 3-4 more times
  13. Slice and serve!
