Deviled Eggs

Description

Hard boiled eggs are stuffed with a creamy blend of mayonnaise and spices.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon fresh dill (optional)
  • 1 pinch paprika

Steps

  1. Slice eggs in half and scoop egg yolks into a bowl. Set egg whites aside.
  2. Mash together egg yolk, mayonnaise, vinegar, dijon mustard, garlic powder, salt, and dill (optional). Mix well.
  3. Stuff egg yolk mixture into egg whites. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate until serving.
