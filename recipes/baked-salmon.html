Baked Salmon

Description

A simple recipe for oven baked salmon with a delicious garlic and lemon marinade.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper

Steps

  1. Mix olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, parsley, basil, salt and pepper in a bowl
  2. Marinate salmon with mixture in a container in fridge for at least 1 hour
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  4. Place salmon on aluminum foil on a pan, spoon marinade on top, and bake for 20 minutes
